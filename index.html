Our Philosophy

Every gown is a love letter

Founded in Paris in 2009 for brides who refused to compromise.

Each piece is cut to order, hand-finished by a single artisan, and arrives with a care card bearing their name. The time it takes to make one gown hasn't changed since our first.

Fifteen years on, that philosophy remains intact — only our reach has grown. We now dress couples across 42 countries, always with the same unhurried intention we brought to the very first commission.
